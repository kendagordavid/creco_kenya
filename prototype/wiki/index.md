# CRECO PBO Act Knowledge Wiki

This wiki compiles approved PBO Act materials into structured, reviewable pages. Unlike RAG systems that retrieve raw document chunks at query time, knowledge is **compiled once** into interlinked wiki pages that CRECO staff can review and update.

## Topics

- [[what-is-a-pbo]] — Definition and characteristics of a Public Benefit Organization
- [[registration-requirements]] — Documents and criteria needed to register
- [[registration-process-and-timeline]] — How registration works and time limits
- [[objects-and-purpose-of-the-act]] — Why the PBO Act exists and what it aims to achieve
- [[pbo-regulatory-authority]] — The Authority's role and public registry

## How this prototype works

1. **Compile** — Source PDFs are transformed into structured Markdown wiki pages (with staff review in production).
2. **Search** — User questions are matched to relevant wiki pages using full-text search.
3. **Answer** — Responses are drawn from compiled wiki content with citations to both wiki pages and original source documents.
