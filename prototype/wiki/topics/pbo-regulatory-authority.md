# The PBO Regulatory Authority

The **Public Benefit Organizations Regulatory Authority (PBORA)** is the body established under the PBO Act to oversee registration and regulation of PBOs in Kenya.

## Key functions

- **Receiving and processing** registration applications
- **Maintaining a public registry** of registered PBOs accessible via the internet
- **Regulating** PBO operations within the framework of the Act
- **Supporting** capacity building, dispute resolution, and information for PBOs

## Public registry

The Authority must maintain a registry that allows the public to verify which organizations are registered PBOs. Website: [pbora.go.ke](https://www.pbora.go.ke)

## Reporting experiences

PBOs that experience registration delays, administrative barriers, or other issues with the Authority can report these through CRECO Kenya's monitoring platform (full platform feature). This helps document civic space conditions for advocacy.
